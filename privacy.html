Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PromptGenius ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit and use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

Information Provided by You

When you use PromptGenius, we do NOT store your prompts or search queries. However, we may collect:

  • Contact information (name, email) if you contact us through our contact form
  • Feedback or comments you voluntarily provide

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand site usage patterns
  • Improve user experience
  • Deliver targeted advertising (see Cookies & Advertising section)

2. Third-Party APIs and Services

OpenRouter API

PromptGenius uses the OpenRouter API (powered by Mistral AI) to generate prompts. When you submit a prompt:

  • Your input prompt is sent to OpenRouter's servers
  • The API processes your request and returns a generated prompt
  • We do NOT store your prompts after generation
  • OpenRouter may retain logs for service improvement (subject to their Privacy Policy)
  • You should review OpenRouter's Privacy Policy

Analytics Services

We use analytics tools to understand how users interact with our website:

  • Google Analytics for traffic analysis
  • These services use cookies and pixels to track your activity
  • Data is used only for improving our service
  • Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized

Advertising Partners

PromptGenius uses advertising services including Google AdSense and Adsterra to support free access:

  • Ads are displayed on our website
  • Ad networks may use cookies and pixels for targeted advertising
  • We do NOT sell your personal data to advertisers
  • You can control ad personalization in your browser settings
  • Review Google's Privacy Policy and Adsterra's Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Your Information

✓ To provide and maintain our service

✓ To improve and optimize the user experience

✓ To analyze usage patterns and trends

✓ To respond to your inquiries and support requests

✓ To comply with legal obligations

✓ To show relevant advertisements

✓ To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Cookies & Advertising

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand user behavior
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by AdSense and Adsterra for targeted ads

How to Control Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings to disable or delete cookies
  • Browser extensions that block tracking
  • Opting out of personalized advertising in Google settings

Advertising Disclosures

We display Google AdSense and Adsterra advertisements to support free access to PromptGenius. These ads may be:

  • Targeted based on your interests and browsing history
  • Delivered by their respective ad networks
  • Subject to their privacy policies and terms

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for data in transit
  • Secure servers hosted by trusted providers
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Exercise data portability rights
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@promptgenius.com

7. Data Retention

We Retain Information:

  • Prompts: Not stored (processed and deleted immediately)
  • Contact Form Data: Retained until issue is resolved
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 26 months
  • Server Logs: Retained for up to 30 days

8. Children's Privacy

PromptGenius is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will delete such information immediately.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information to us should contact us at contact@promptgenius.com

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of PromptGenius constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: contact@promptgenius.com

Website: PromptGenius.com
